<B>LAN MapShot™</B>
<P>
Automatically discover & map your switched network in minutes! Fire up this application and it quickly discovers network devices.  Working in tandem with Microsoft Visio™, LAN MapShot™ instantly locates IPX servers, IP and NetBIOS devices on your network, pops up maps showing slot and port level connections and visual routes through switches.  Plus it pinpoints printers, routers, switches, managed hubs and hosts.  It even flags port speed mismatches and shows you where your Fluke Networks tool’s are located.
